About

I am a chess master and senior data engineer from Azerbaijan, living in Boston, Massachusetts. With over 8 years of experience in business intelligence and cloud architecture, I specialize in turning complex data into clear decisions — using Power BI, Python, SQL, AWS, and Azure.

Beyond my engineering career, I founded Dom Chess Academy and Dom Tech Academy to guide young people in both chess strategy and technology careers. I have been recognized by universities and organizations for my contributions to chess and STEM education, and I represented students at the United Nations Commission.

"Chess and data engineering share the same core skill: seeing patterns others miss."

I also developed the Dom Chess App and am building Nurnex — a platform connecting tech graduates with recruiters through portfolio-building support and bootcamp training.
