About

I am a chess master and senior data engineer from Azerbaijan, living in the Greater Boston area (Waltham, MA). With over 8 years of experience in business intelligence and cloud architecture, I specialize in turning complex data into clear decisions using Power BI, Python, SQL, AWS, and Azure.

Beyond my engineering career, I founded Dom Chess Academy and Dom Tech Academy — both located at 303 Wyman St, Suite 300, Waltham, MA 02451 — to guide young people in both chess strategy and technology careers.

I have been recognized by multiple universities and chess organizations for my contributions to chess and STEM education, and I represented students at the United Nations Commission. Both academies operate across two Greater Boston locations: Waltham and Everett, MA.
